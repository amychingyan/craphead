Entertaining Every Second
The title of this exhibition comes from a Nam June Paik quote: “I am a poor [wo]man from a poor country, so I have to be entertaining every second.” The works in “Entertaining Every Second” follow our inquiry into colonialism in Asia and North America through personal experience and ancestral history.

Beautiful Canvas Shoes “Until Either One Closes”: an agreement between a gallery (aka artist-run, Saskatoon) and the restaurant next door (Jin Jin Cuisine) for all openings at the gallery to be catered by Jin Jin, for the rest of time, either one of the businesses closes. We also asked for the logo of Jin Jin Cuisine to be added to the logo block of aka artist-run.

Truck Contemporary Art, with M:ST Biennial, Calgary, Sept. 7 – Oct. 20, 2018
